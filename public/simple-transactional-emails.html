OhMySMTP: Simple Transactional Emails for your app, website or service

Why OhMySMTP

The goal of OhMySMTP is to provide you with a fast and easy way to send Transactional Emails, with a modern, easy to use dashboard, simple API and easy to integrate application libraries.

You can send email with a single HTTPS API call, pull in a library from our supported integrations or even use SMTP. We focus on getting users setup quickly and out of the way so you can focus on building your service. Let's take a closer look

Getting Started

When you first sign up for OhMySMTP, we'll take you through a simple Onboarding process. Four easy steps:

  • Setup an organization - a group of domain names you can send from. It means you can separate your different services or companies that have multiple domains or logins to the dashboard
  • Enter your first domain - provide a URL and give it a name
  • Verify your domain - instructions for verifying your domain over DKIM. This is vital to prove ownership of your domain, increase deliverability and prevent spam
  • Choose a plan - pick the right plan for your Organization

Sending your first email

Once inside OhMySMTP, we include examples in multiple code languages, such as Ruby, Python, Go, PHP, and even include examples for sending direct from the command line using Curl

And we have a full step by step guide for sending your first email in our comprehensive documentation

Tracking your emails

Emails sometimes hard or soft-bounce, and some can even be marked as Spam by our outgoing spam filter. We provide an easy to use dashboard to see a summary of your sent emails. You can search for individual emails, see their status and drill down on them. We store 30-days of email history
