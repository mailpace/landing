Transactional Only and Developer Friendly

Transactional Only

OhMySMTP can only be used for Transactional Email - think password resets, notifications and welcome emails.

These are your most important emails - don't use other providers that mix them up with slow, bulk marketing emails and risk being funnelled straight into spam folders

Simple Integrations & API

Forget DKIM, SPF and the archaic email ecosystem, send emails using our simple API and client libraries. Or integrate almost any app through our SMTP server

Our carbon-neutral, global infrastructure runs 24/7 and is optimized for maximum delivery speed

Get to the inbox, fast

OhMySMTP only allows transactional emails, from verified senders. We actively remove spam accounts and only legitimate emails can be sent through our platform.

The end result is that email gets to your user's inbox, every time

By developers, for developers

We created OhMySMTP to make life easier for developers. After years of experience building apps with email we wanted something that took minutes to set up, covered all the bases and just worked.

Eco-friendly, Ethical and Privacy-focused

Eco-friendly

  • Our email infrastructure runs on 100% carbon-neutral, renewable energy powered servers

We put your users first

  • No read tracking - we only check if the receiving server accepted the email, we never invade a person's inbox privacy.
  • No spam - we enforce DKIM validation, actively remove spammers and automatically check all outbound emails for spam

A software company run by real people

We are fully independent and self-funded. We're a small business, not a major corporation backed by billionaires or unscrupulous investors
